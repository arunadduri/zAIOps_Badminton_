Tournament Rules

🏸 BADMINTON TOURNAMENT RULES (KNOCKOUT FORMAT)

Based on Badminton World Federation standards

1️⃣ TOURNAMENT FORMAT

  • Type: Knockout (Elimination)
  • Lose → eliminated
  • Winner advances to next round

2️⃣ MATCH FORMAT

  • Match = Best of 3 games
  • Each game = 21 points
  • Rally scoring system

Winning Rules:

  • First to 21 points
  • Must win by 2 points
  • At 20–20 → continue until 2-point lead
  • Max cap = 30 points (30–29)

3️⃣ CATEGORIES (ALL 5 FORMATS)

  • 👨 Men Singles (1 vs 1)
  • 👩 Women Singles (1 vs 1)
  • 👨👨 Men Doubles (2 vs 2)
  • 👩👩 Women Doubles (2 vs 2)
  • 👨👩 Mixed Doubles (2 vs 2, male + female)

4️⃣ COURT LINES & VALID AREAS

Court markings include:

  • Outer sidelines (full width)
  • Inner sidelines (singles width)
  • Baseline (back boundary)
  • Short service line
  • Long service line (only for doubles serve)
  • Center line

👨👩 SINGLES – VALID AREAS

During Rally:

  • Width → Inner sidelines
  • Length → Full court (baseline included)

✅ Valid: Inside inner sidelines, Up to baseline

❌ Fault: Outside inner sidelines, Beyond baseline

👨👨 / 👩👩 / 👨👩 DOUBLES – VALID AREAS

During Rally:

  • Width → Outer sidelines (full width)
  • Length → Full court (baseline included)

✅ Valid: Full court

❌ Fault: Outside outer sidelines, Beyond baseline

5️⃣ SERVICE RULES

General Rules:

  • Serve must be diagonal
  • Shuttle hit below waist
  • Racket pointing downward
  • Feet inside service court
  • Feet stationary

👨👩 Singles – Service Area:

  • Width → Inner sidelines
  • Length → Short service line → Baseline

👨👨 / 👩👩 / 👨👩 Doubles – Service Area:

  • Width → Outer sidelines
  • Length → Short service line → Long service line
  • 👉 Baseline is NOT valid for serve

6️⃣ SERVING POSITIONS

Singles:

  • Even score → serve from right
  • Odd score → serve from left

Doubles / Mixed:

  • Even score → serve from right
  • Odd score → serve from left

Rotation Rules:

  • One serve per side
  • If serving side wins: Same server continues, Players switch sides
  • If receiving side wins: They gain serve

7️⃣ RALLY RULES

  • Shuttle must cross the net
  • Shuttle landing on line = IN
  • Rally ends when: Shuttle hits ground, Fault occurs

8️⃣ FAULTS

  • Shuttle outside valid boundaries
  • Serve outside correct service area
  • Shuttle fails to cross net
  • Shuttle hits obstruction
  • Player touches net
  • Double hit / carry
  • Illegal serve

9️⃣ LET (REPLAY)

  • Shuttle stuck on net
  • Receiver not ready
  • External disturbance

🔟 INTERVALS

  • 60 sec break at 11 points
  • 2 min between games
  • Change sides: After each game, In 3rd game at 11 points
