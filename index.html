Badminton Tournament Rules

Knockout Format • BWF Standards

🏆 Format 🎯 Scoring 👥 Categories 📏 Court 🏸 Service ⚡ Gameplay
🏆

Tournament Format

Knockout Single Elimination
  • → Lose once = Eliminated
  • → Winner advances to next round
  • → No second chances
🎯

Match Format & Scoring

Best of 3 Rally Scoring
  • → Each game to 21 points
  • → Point on every rally
🔥 Must win by 2 points
⚡ At 20-20: Play continues until 2-point lead
🏁 Maximum cap: 30 points (30-29 wins)
👥

Tournament Categories

👨 Men's Singles 1 vs 1
👩 Women's Singles 1 vs 1
👨👨 Men's Doubles 2 vs 2
👩👩 Women's Doubles 2 vs 2
👨👩 Mixed Doubles 2 vs 2
📏

Court Lines & Valid Areas

Singles Play

  • ✓ Width: Inner sidelines
  • ✓ Length: Full court to baseline
✅ Inside inner sidelines
❌ Outside boundaries

Doubles Play

  • ✓ Width: Outer sidelines (full width)
  • ✓ Length: Full court to baseline
🏸

Service Rules

⚡ Serve must be diagonal
⚡ Hit shuttle below waist
⚡ Racket pointing downward

Singles Service

  • → Inner sidelines
  • → Short service line to baseline

Doubles Service

  • → Outer sidelines
  • → Short to long service line
  • ⚠️ Baseline NOT valid

Serving Positions

Even Score Serve from right
Odd Score Serve from left
⚡

Gameplay & Faults

Rally Rules

  • ✓ Shuttle must cross net
  • ✓ Landing on line = IN
  • ✓ Rally ends when shuttle hits ground

Common Faults

  • ❌ Shuttle outside boundaries
  • ❌ Fails to cross net
  • ❌ Player touches net
  • ❌ Double hit or carry
  • ❌ Illegal serve

Let (Replay)

  • 🔄 Shuttle stuck on net
  • 🔄 Receiver not ready
  • 🔄 External disturbance

Intervals & Breaks

60 sec Break at 11 points
2 min Between games
